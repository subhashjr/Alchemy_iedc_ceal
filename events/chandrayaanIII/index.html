CHANDRAYAAN - III

14/07/2023 • A New Chapter in India's Space Journey Live Screening - ended


Attingal: The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC) club of College of Engineering Attingal is screening the live footage of the take-off of Chandrayan-III on Friday (14/7/2023) at 2 PM in SDPK Hall (ASAP Cell). All students are expected to take part in witnessing the next major space travel operation of the nation.

Also there were talks conducted by Govind SR about the technical aspects of the mission and about the future of space travel.
